About the Aero Club

The **Aero Club of MACE** is a hub for aspiring engineers passionate about flight, aeromodelling, and drone technology. Founded with the vision to integrate theory with hands-on experimentation, the club focuses on designing, fabricating, and testing aircraft for national and international competitions such as the **SAE Drone Development Competition (DDC)**.

Members gain experience in aircraft aerodynamics, propulsion systems, control algorithms, and lightweight material design. From conceptual sketches to successful flight demonstrations, the Aero Club proudly represents MACE’s technical prowess and innovation in aerial platforms.
